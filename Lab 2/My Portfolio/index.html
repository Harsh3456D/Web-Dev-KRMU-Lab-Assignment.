About Me

Hello! I'm Harsh Dabas, a passionate full-stack developer with a love for creating innovative digital solutions. With experience in web development and database management, I specialize in building responsive, user-friendly applications that solve real-world problems for students and communities.

My journey in technology started with a curiosity about how websites work, and it has evolved into a career dedicated to continuous learning and improvement. I enjoy working with both front-end and back-end technologies, always striving to write clean, efficient code that makes a difference in people's daily lives.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to student-focused projects, or brainstorming solutions for campus-related challenges. I believe in the power of technology to connect people and solve practical problems.
